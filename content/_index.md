I'm an AI Research Engineer at [Helsing](https://helsing.ai/), building safeguard systems and evaluation infrastructure for AI deployed in high-stakes environments. I co-founded [Future Frame](https://github.com/edadaltocg/futureframe/) in 2024 — a startup building foundation models for tabular data, backed by [Entrepreneur First](https://www.joinef.com/) and [Transpose](https://www.transposeplatform.vc/).

I hold a PhD in Machine Learning from [CentraleSupélec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr/) — [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/), where my research addressed AI reliability through out-of-distribution detection, uncertainty quantification, and selective prediction, supervised by [Pablo Piantanida](https://sites.google.com/mila.quebec/pablo-piantanida/home) and funded by [BPI France](https://www.bpifrance.com/) and [IBM Research](https://research.ibm.com/). I held a research position at [Mila](https://mila.quebec/en/) in Montreal in 2022, and hold degrees from [ISAE-SUPAERO](https://www.isae-supaero.fr/en/) (MSc, Toulouse) and the [Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica](https://ita.br/) (BE, Brazil).
