I'm an AI Research Engineer at [Helsing](https://helsing.ai/), a new type of defence company delivering AI-enabled autonomous systems across all domains to protect our democracies. My work focuses on scalable evaluation of AI products, uncertainty quantification and calibration, and safety mechanisms that ensure AI operates reliably in real-world and adversarial environments. I co-founded [Future Frame](https://github.com/edadaltocg/futureframe/) in 2024 — a startup building foundation models for tabular data, backed by [Entrepreneur First](https://www.joinef.com/) and [Transpose](https://www.transposeplatform.vc/).

I hold a PhD in Machine Learning from [CentraleSupélec](https://www.centralesupelec.fr/) — [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/), where I published research into making AI safer to operate in real-life applications, focusing on out-of-distribution detection, uncertainty quantification, and selective prediction, supervised by [Pablo Piantanida](https://sites.google.com/mila.quebec/pablo-piantanida/home) and funded by [BPI France](https://www.bpifrance.com/) and [IBM Research](https://research.ibm.com/). I did a research internship at [Mila](https://mila.quebec/en/) in Montreal in 2022, working on uncertainty quantification for medical image segmentation. I hold an MSc in Applied Mathematics and Data Science from [ISAE-SUPAERO](https://www.isae-supaero.fr/en/) in Toulouse, and a BE in Aerospace Engineering from the [Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica](https://ita.br/) in Brazil.

As a side project, I co-created [Prompt Riddle](https://promptriddle.com) in 2018 — an online enigma-solving game with over 5,000 players, where the goal is to escape an emulated terminal by outsmarting an AI.

Broadly, I am interested in developing robust general purpose AI models.
